Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju

Summary

Popularly known as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works mainly in Telugu cinema industry. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema. In the year 2015, Prabhas starred in the movie, Baahubali, which is one of the highest grossing movies of Indian cinema and later reprised his titular role in Baahubali 2:The Conclusion (2017), which became the first Indian film in the history of Indian cinema to gross ₹1000 crores at the box office.

Career

2002–2011:

Prabhas started his film career with Eeswar (2002). In 2003, he was the lead in Raghavendra.

2012–2014

Prabhas starred in Rebel, an action film directed by Raghava Lawrence.
